Terminology¶

  • Black Magic Debug (aka. BMD): The whole project name, can also refer to the Firmware

  • Black Magic Probe (aka. BMP): The dedicated aka. “native” hardware that runs the BMD firmware. Other target platforms are also supported, like various debuggers built into development boards like st-link for example.

  • Black Magic Debup App (aka. BMDA): The BMD firmware compiled as a stand alone application instead of a firmware. It can be used as a “software in the middle”
